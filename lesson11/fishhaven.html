Upcoming Events in Fish Haven

Founders Day Fresco

by Janica Juarez

Family time fishing by the lake, what could be better? Our yearly Founder's Day celebration provides fishing and more. Every year our town celebrates big by having a parade, youth dance, games at the park, carnival, baby contest, and art shows to name a few. Families near and far love coming together to celebrate. This year to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Fish Haven becoming a town three local artists have been invited to create a Founder's Day Fresco in the town hall. Our town's love of fish will of course be a highlight in the fresco. Bob Ross, Leonardo di Vinci, and Frida Kahlo have been hard at working collaborating on this piece of art they will be adding soon. Leo was overheard saying, "A painter should begin every canvas with a wash of black, because all things in nature are dark except where exposed by the light." If you're a little nervous about how our fresco will turn out with a wash of black first, well we are too. But as Bob Ross reminded us, "We don't make mistakes. We just have happy accidents." With the three of them working together, it will be a tribute to remember.
