Pancakes in the Park

by Pamela Patterson

The Preston tradition of pancakes in the park began 30 years ago when the local Boy Scout Troop was trying to raise funds for their yearly activities. Since then it has grown to a citywide weekly gathering. Donations are welcomed and go to local charities and non-profit organizations. Local citizens have positive attitudes towards this weekly gathering. Any opportunity to come together as a community is welcomed in a small town. Many enjoy the chance to strengthen friendships, and young kids love the chance to be outside with so many friends. Businesses donate most of the supplies and food which allows all the proceeds to be donated to those in need. Preston really knocks it out of the park with their weekly pancake gatherings.
