The beautiful Salt Lake Temple is located in downtown Salt Lake City, Utah. It offers beautiful grounds to walk around and enjoy. There is so much to do nearby, it's the perfect location for a family vacation. Visit the nearby stores for wonderful shopping experiences. Check out the Beehive House and other historical sites to learn more about the early Saints that settled this area. Of course, you can't forget to visit the new Conference Center and see the views from it's beautiful garden rooftop. If you're looking for a family getaway with an opportunity to attend the temple, look no further!
