Site Name

Weather Done Better: serving Southeast Idaho

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide the current weather conditions for Southeastern Idaho. It will also provide a forecast for cities including Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It will serve as a community resource to keep citizens informed of upcoming events. There will also be a form to collect storm information from users.
